Round to 2 decimals.

VLEN

To calculate the length of a vector, we first calculate the dot product of the vector with itself by multiplying each element in \color{#6495ED}{\vec a} with itself, then we sum all the products.
The length of the vector is the square root of that sum.

Thus, we do \sqrt{\sum\limits_{i=0}^{n} \color{#6495ED}{\vec a}_i \times \color{#6495ED}{\vec a}_i} where n = vector length.

For \color{#6495ED}{\vec a} that is \sqrt{showDot(VA,VA)}

That is \sqrt{showHalfdot(VA,VA)}

= \sqrt{DOT}

The square root of DOT is VLEN
